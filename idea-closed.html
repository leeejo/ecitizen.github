Overview

Take Up the Mobile/Digital Services Ideas Challenge

Let your ideas spark!

We are looking for ideas to create more user-centric mobile or digital services that can allow users to easily transact with the Government from a mobile device or desktop browser anytime, anywhere.

Many government services are already offered as mobile or digital services today. Could these services be better designed to be:

  • Combined into a single mobile or digital service for greater convenience or
  • Optimised on the mobile platform when they are traditionally only available on the web or
  • Offered as a new mobile / digital service

Tell us how to better meet your needs in transacting with the Government, especially in situations when you have to deal with multiple agencies at one time. Submit your ideas now and stand to win attractive prizes!

Guidelines

Tips for to Make Your Ideas Stand Out

We are looking for Originality, Usefulness and Value of the idea to citizens. If your potential mobile/digital services ideas involve two or more government agencies, you may gain higher points.

Describe the Current Scenario:

Do your research and assess areas that need improvements, including which government agencies could be involved. For example,

  • What areas of mobile/digital services could be improved?
  • What are the online services that are currently not available on mobile devices?
  • What are the services currently not available as a mobile/digital service?

Describe your Idea:

Provide more details of your idea. Tell us the intent, features to have and elaborate how these should work. Do also include which government agencies could be involved.

Apply your Ideas

Your idea can span across any domain, from transport, health, housing, environment, education, family, to social/community etc.

Win us over with the Benefits:

Note down all the positive points of your ideas and tell us how you think your new mobile/digital services can benefit both citizens and the government agencies.

Submission

A picture tells a thousand words

You may attach images, presentation slides or videos to illustrate your idea. The clearer your idea, the more points you get! You may also attach a text document to further elaborate your idea.

Judging Criteria

Idea submissions will be assessed by a judging panel with expertise in the Singapore public sector and voting by the public. The organiser acting on advice from the Judges, reserve the right to disqualify any entry that, in its opinion, violates the rules or the spirit of the competition. All prizes are awarded based on a weightage of both the judging panel and number of votes by the public.

Winners will be notified via email provided during submission. Prizes are non-transferrable. The prizes will automatically be forfeited if unclaimed within the time given in the notice to the participant. The organiser reserves the right in deciding whether the winning idea will be implemented eventually.

Vote for your favourite ideas!

Your votes will help us determine the top three winners for the challenges.

An all in one feedback app

Submitted by piscesfishie, 8 months ago

12 votes

Have a mobile app to make it more convenient for members of the public to send their feedback on municipal issues in Singapore.

It should be easier and faster for the public to provide their feedback on municipal issues on one common platform, based on the issues they encounter. The app will automatically route each feedback to the relevant agency so that more timely service and response can be provided.
